Privacy Policy for NityaAbhyasa: Study Planner

Effective date: June 26, 2026
Last updated: June 26, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how NityaAbhyasa: Study Planner handles information when you use the App.
Contact email: bloomcycleofficial@gmail.com

NityaAbhyasa is a study-planning and productivity app that helps users manage tasks, routines, exams, focus sessions, reminders, goals, streaks and achievements.

The current version:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not offer subscriptions or in-app purchases
- Does not use analytics or crash-reporting services
- Does not upload study information to developer-controlled servers
- Stores study information locally on the user's device

2. Information stored in the App

You may voluntarily enter information such as:
- Name or preferred display name
- Course or study goal
- Subjects and tasks
- Study plans and routines
- Examination names and dates
- Notes and reminders
- Focus-session history
- Daily study targets
- Streaks, XP, badges and achievements
- Notification, appearance and App preferences

This information is used only to provide the App's features and is stored locally on your device.

Optional fields may be left blank.

Avoid entering sensitive personal information that is not necessary for study planning.

3. Accounts and personal information
   
NityaAbhyasa does not create online user accounts.

The App does not directly request:
- Phone numbers
- Postal addresses
- Contact lists
- Precise location
- Photos, videos or microphone recordings
- Health or biometric information
- Banking or payment information
- Government identification information
- Passwords or authentication credentials

Because no online account is created, there is no NityaAbhyasa account to delete.

4. Backup and restore

NityaAbhyasa allows you to create and restore a .nityaabhyasa backup file.

A backup may contain the study information and preferences stored in the App.

Backup creation and restoration are initiated by you through Android's system file picker. You choose where the file is saved or which file is restored.

You may save a backup to:
- Your device
- Removable storage
- Google Drive
- Another storage provider available through the system picker

NityaAbhyasa does not automatically upload backup files to servers controlled by us.

If you use a third-party storage provider, that provider handles the file according to its own privacy policy.

Backup files may contain personal study information. Store them securely and share them only with people you trust.

Deleting App data or uninstalling NityaAbhyasa does not automatically delete backup files saved outside the App.

5. Deleting App data

You can delete locally stored information by:
- Deleting individual records inside the App
- Using Delete all app data in Settings
- Clearing the App's storage through Android settings
- Uninstalling the App

Deleting all app data may permanently remove tasks, routines, exams, notes, focus history, progress, achievements and preferences.

Because the information is not stored on our servers, we generally cannot recover it after deletion.

Backup files saved outside the App must be deleted separately.

6. Feedback and support

The App may include a feedback option that opens your email application.

No message is sent unless you choose to send it.

If you contact us, we may receive:
- Your email address
- Your name, if included
- Your message
- Screenshots or attachments
- Device, Android or App-version details you choose to provide

This information is used only to respond to feedback, investigate technical issues and improve the App.

Do not send passwords, payment details, government identification numbers or other unnecessary sensitive information.

You may request deletion of feedback correspondence by contacting us.

7. Permissions and device features

NityaAbhyasa may use the following Android features:

Notifications
Used for study reminders, routine alerts, exam reminders, focus timers and break timers.
You can disable notifications through Android settings.

Alarms and reminders
Used to deliver alerts at times selected by you.
Some Android versions may require special alarm access.

Device restart access
Used to restore scheduled reminders after the device restarts.

File picker access
Used only when you choose to create or restore a backup file.
The App accesses only the file or destination you select.

Internet access
May be used to open:
- This Privacy Policy
- Support links
- Your email application
- Google Play pages
- Other links selected by you

Your study information is not automatically uploaded to us merely because the App has internet access.

8. Advertising, analytics and payments

The current version of NityaAbhyasa does not use:
- Advertising SDKs
- Personalised advertising
- Behavioural tracking
- Firebase Analytics
- Google Analytics
- Firebase Crashlytics
- Third-party user analytics
- Google Play Billing

If these features are added later, this Privacy Policy and the Google Play Data Safety declaration will be updated.

9. Data sharing and security

We do not sell, rent or trade personal information.

Your locally stored study information is not shared with advertisers, analytics providers or data brokers.

Information may be processed independently by Android, Google Play, email providers, storage providers or device manufacturers when you use their services.

We take reasonable steps to minimise unnecessary data handling. However, no device or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

You should:
- Protect your device with a screen lock
- Keep Android and the App updated
- Store backup files securely
- Avoid sharing an unlocked device
- Avoid entering unnecessary sensitive information

10. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children through accounts, advertising systems, analytics systems or developer-controlled servers.

A parent or guardian who believes that a child has sent personal information through feedback email may contact us to request deletion.
